Shravan Mayilan
Orlando, FL

Shravan is a talented young artist who lives with his mom, dad and sister in Orlando, FL. He is overcoming his speech and social skills deficits by creating art. He loves the sensory nature of art and his favorite medium is acrylic. Shravan enjoys painting still-life and landscapes.
